Overview of features

  1. Dashboard: The welcome screen offers quick access to all vehicles. Locate the selected vehicle current location, ignition status, remaining fuel, safety score, and battery health. Check trip history for the selected vehicle and add new vehicles easily.
  2. Vehicle Selector: Choose from multiple vehicles linked to your account to view specific DRIVECO information.
  3. Quick and Easy Navigation: Use the side menu for seamless navigation through the app's features.
  4. Trip History: Review past trips taken in your vehicle
  5. My Trips: Get a daily overview of vehicle activity, including driver safety scores and harsh events.
  6. Weekly Summary: View weekly trip statistics. Compare driving metrics for travel distance, travel time, or fuel consumption. Download detailed reports.
  7. Check Fuel Summary: Get a quick overview of remaining fuel and mileage details specific to the current user.
  8. Fuel Analytics: Explore weekly or monthly trends for fuel consumption, mileage, and fuel savings
  9. Track Harsh Events: Review harsh events and safety alerts to identify driving patterns and areas for improvement.
  10. Monitor Vehicle Health: Find information critical to vehicle health, including battery level, engine oil, fuel level, coolant level, and days until service.
  11. View Vehicle Information: Access vehicle details such as make, model, year, recent harsh events, and trip statistics.
  12. View and Edit User Information: View and update user details and images associated with the selected vehicle by clicking
  13. Live Alerts: Receive live notifications for specific vehicle events.
  14. Safety: Alerts Focus on crucial notifications related to driving behavior, such as harsh braking, acceleration, and overspeeding.
  15. App Settings: Update geofence details, set thresholds for overspeeding, idling, minimum trip distance, and low fuel levels. Manage curfews and alerts.
  16. Help: Report bugs or speak with a DRIVCO expert for assistance.
  17. Find My Vehicle: Locate your vehicle on the map and get an estimated time to reach it from your current location.
